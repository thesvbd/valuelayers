DCA Simulator for S&P 500

What is DCA?

Dollar-Cost Averaging (DCA) is an investment strategy where you invest a fixed amount of money at regular intervals, regardless of the asset's price. This approach can help reduce the impact of volatility on your investments.

This simulator uses historical S&P 500 data from 1928 to 2021 to demonstrate how a DCA strategy would have performed over different time periods.
