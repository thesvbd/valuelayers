Risk-Reward Ratio Calculator

About the Calculator

Purpose:

Helps investors evaluate the potential risk and reward of a trading opportunity.

Usage:

Enter the stock purchase price, expected profit price, and the price at which you would accept a loss.

Output:

The calculator computes the risk-reward ratio in a 1:X format, where X represents the potential profit in relation to the potential loss.

Interpretation:

The higher the value of X, the more attractive the trading opportunity is in terms of risk-reward ratio.
