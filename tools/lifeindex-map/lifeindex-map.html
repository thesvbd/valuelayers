LifeIndex Map

Discover where your income takes you from Ramen to Rolex

What is LifeIndex Map?

A fun tool that shows you approximate costs of living around the world based on your income level. Please note that these are averaged estimates - for accurate, up-to-date prices, we recommend checking NomadList or Numbeo for detailed cost breakdowns in specific locations.

Our Lifestyle Categories

Caveman Life 🦇: Absolute minimum living standards focused on basic survival needs

Student Life 📚: Basic shared accommodation with minimal amenities and budget-conscious lifestyle

Digital Nomad 💻: Comfortable solo living with modern essentials and remote work capabilities

Tech Bro Life 🏢: High-end modern living with premium amenities and exclusive access

Crypto Millionaire 🚀: Ultra-luxury lifestyle with top-tier housing and premium services

How We Calculate This

We combine your active and passive income, then compare it with real cost-of-living data from cities worldwide. We factor in housing, food, transportation, and entertainment costs to determine where you can maintain your desired lifestyle.
