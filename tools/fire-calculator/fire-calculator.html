What is F.I.R.E.?

F.I.R.E. (Financial Independence, Retire Early) is a financial strategy and lifestyle focused on achieving financial independence and the possibility of early retirement. The basic principles are:

  • Maximizing savings (often 50-70% of income)
  • Minimizing expenses
  • Aggressive investing in a diversified portfolio
  • Creating passive income

The 4% Rule

The 4% rule is a commonly used estimate in the FIRE community. It assumes that you can safely withdraw 4% of your investment portfolio each year without depleting your savings during retirement. This means that to achieve financial independence, you need to accumulate 25 times your annual expenses. However, this percentage can be adjusted according to individual needs and risk tolerance.
